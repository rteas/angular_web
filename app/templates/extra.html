Game: Blastemy (prototype)

Play as a pilot on a mission to intercept a monster invasion!

Swiftly dodge enemies and their bullets while launching attacks of your own!

Get cool power ups and complete your mission by defeating the boss!

Updates:

Added versions for multi-OS support

Please let me know (via message) if there are any issues!
